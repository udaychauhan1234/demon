About us

At Awesome Event Pvt Ltd, we don’t just plan events — we create experiences that resonate, inspire, and leave a lasting impression. With our roots in Delhi NCR, we proudly serve a diverse clientele across corporate, educational, and social sectors. Whether it’s a high-profile conference, a school’s annual function, or an elegant private celebration, we bring unmatched energy, style, and precision to every project. Our identity is bold and regal, symbolized by the lion — representing strength, leadership, and excellence. With a touch of luxury in our signature black and gold theme, we aim to make every event a showstopper.
